2) Explain Data Python Types?

The python data types include intigers, floats, and strings. Intigers are whole numbers or numbers without decimals such as -2, -1, 0, 1, 2. Floats on the other hand are numbers that contain decimals such as 1/3 or sqrt(2). Strings on the other hand are sets of numbers that can be ordered or unordered.
